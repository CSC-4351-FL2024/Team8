Parking Information

Here you can find information about each parking deck/lot:

  • T DECK: Located across from 55 Park Place this is the ideal location to park if you have class in aderhold, CMII, or 55 park place.
  • M DECK: Located across from Student Center East this is the ideal location to park if you want to grab a quick bite in the cafeteria or if you have class in Student Center West or Sparks Hall.
  • N DECK: Located across from Petit Science Center this is the ideal location to park if you would like to go to the recreation center or have class in Petit Science Center and Urban Life.
  • S DECK: Located across from Grady Hospital this is the ideal location to park if you are a nursing student and have clinicals.
  • BLUE LOT: Located near GSU Stadium this parking deck occasionally opens when events occur in Green lot. It is currently reserved for staff.
  • GREEN LOT: Located near Yugo student housing and the GSU Stadium this is the ideal location to park for free and attend GSU football games.
